How to Play

Use this page to learn the game flow and how to spot phishing, email fraud, and suspicious inbox activity.

1. Read the message carefully

Check the sender name, subject line, and email body for anything that seems off or urgent.

2. Check links and email addresses

Hover over links before you click them and compare the sender domain to the company it claims to be from.

3. Watch for fraud triggers

Be suspicious of requests for money, passwords, account verification, or personal details.

4. Choose your action

Decide whether to reply, archive, delete, or report based on the message content.

Tip: Legitimate companies never ask you to share your password by email. When in doubt, pause and verify.
